Butter Chicken

Description

Noodles with a spicy Peanut sauce!

Ingredients

  • 1 (9 ounce) package dried udon noodles
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • 1 ½ tablespoons minced fresh ginger root
  • 3 tablespoons soy sauce

Steps

  1. Bring a large pot with lightly salted water to a rolling boil. Drop the udon in a few noodles at a time and return to a boil.
  2. While the udon noodles are cooking, whisk the chicken broth, ginger, soy sauce, peanut butter, honey, chile oil, and garlic in a saucepan over medium-high heat.
